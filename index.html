Work

Business Rules (Software) Engineer Intern - Delta Dental
I interned at Delta Dental during the summer of 2017. Throughout my internship I contributed to the next gen development of their Business Rules software. My tasks included designing and creating the next generation BRIDE Web(BRIDE = Business Rules Interactive Development Environment) and implementing REST services from end to end. While accomplishing those tasks I learned HTML, CSS, Javascript, Typescript, Angular 2+, and how to use Java as a server side language.

My approach to designing BRIDE Web was to keep things as simple and flexible as possible. I organized the web application in a leveled manner because that is how the data I was working with was structured. I also did not rely on any hardcoded data, text or values as I organized the services to provide everything I would need. Speaking of services, I helped organize and design the available REST services to ensure they would result in optimal client and server communication.

One of the largest tasks I tackled in the application was the Test Harness. The Test Harness is an important part of the application that the Business Users utilize to test the business rules that have been created. The Test Harness allows those users to enter values for fields that are specific to the context of the business situation. Since there are many contexts I had to design a way to take in a specification of the current context and construct the Test Harness with the correct input types and fields. And then I also had to extract their input so I could send it to the server to execute the rules against.

I worked with a group of interns and a few Software Engineers. As a team we were constantly working together and helping each other out. Overall I gained invaluable experience designing a large application, learning new skills and working in a fast paced team environment.
